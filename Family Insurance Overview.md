## Guiding Principles

> [!INFO] **Insurance Philosophy**
> 1. **Budget**: Cap total insurance spend at **10% of household income**.
> 2. **Decouple protection from investment**: Buy pure protection (term life, health/IP, PA) separately from savings/investment vehicles. Don't mix — get the best in each.
> 3. **Implication**: Prefer term life over whole life for death/TPD coverage. Use IP + rider for hospitalisation. Invest the premium difference independently.
